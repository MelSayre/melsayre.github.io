Priscorum

Thank you for taking an interest in my adventures!

Currently, I am releasing various works (short stories, novellas, art and photography) on Patreon - it starts at just a dollar a month for various rewards, including rough drafts of larger works, final short stories and novellas, free photography prints and more. Take a look and see what works for you - I greatly appreciate any and all support.

Click here to view the Patreon at patreon.com/melsayre.

That said, even if you’re just admiring something, I’d love to hear from you. Every little bit keeps artists going. Whether we’re doodling, drawing, taking photos or anything else creative - it’s all art and we love to talk about it. Life is our inspiration.

Feel free to email at priscorum@gmail.com with any questions or comments!

I also run a writing Discord server called Scriptum for write-ins, workshops and more: click here to join.
