Welcome to my Yosemite National Park Charity Print Sale, running from 5/22 to 7/05. Proceeds of this sale support the National Park Foundation in its effort to keeping parks across the states healthy and accessible.

Prices are $20 for a 5"x7", 8"x12" for $25, 11"x14" for $35, and 12"x18" for $40, printed on professional lustre Fuji paper. If you want a larger print (16"x20" or 16"x24", particularly) or a different size, please contact me directly at priscorum@gmail.com. If you'd like a different print from the full album (available here), please also let me know. All prices below include shipping domestically - contact me for anything international!

Anything above the 11"x14" will be drop shipped to you directly, with a small envelope with information via USPS. The rest of the prints will have information cards included with the mailing.

If you would like to leave a tip (add it to your shopping cart!), it would go toward the photographer getting a new lens to replace a stolen telephoto lens and exploring more of these glorious areas. In addition, if you'd like to support me on Patreon, I'd appreciate it greatly! Click here to check me out, along with my other writing, photography and art adventures.
